What is a Small Cash Loan?

Small Cash Loan or Instant Personal Loan is a financial product offered by Loanbaba. It is an unsecured short-term personal loan, where you as a borrower can avail an amount between Rs. 10,000 and Rs. 1,00,000 (Rs. One Lakh). This loan facility is only available for salaried individuals. It is a multipurpose loan, and you can use the funds for expenses such as medical emergency, home repair, travel trip, an important festive event, fees for an educational course, purchase of a gadget, and many more. Thus, a small cash loan is a perfect solution to meet all your unexpected expenses and urgent cash needs.

NOTE: Loanbaba is a direct lender of this product. Currently, Small Cash Loan/Instant Personal Loan is available in Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Jaipur, Indore amongst other major cities.

You can simply visit loanbaba.com and apply for Instant Small Cash Loan online.

Small Cash Loan Eligibility Requirements

Below listed are the small cash loan eligibility requirements.

  • You should be at least 23 years of age and a salaried individual to apply for this loan. You should also draw a regular monthly salary.
  • Minimum net monthly salary should be at least Rs. 25,000. The salary must get credited to your bank account. Cash salary is not acceptable.
  • You need to furnish income proof, such as salary slips, and bank account statement reflecting the drawn salary.
  • You should submit KYC documents (ID Proof, Address Proof, PAN card)
  • Minimum credit score should be 600+.

Frequently Asked Questions About Small Cash Loan

Given below are some of the important questions you may have about Loanbaba small cash loan.

  • 1. Do I need to be currently employed to apply for small cash loan?

    Yes, you need to be currently employed and drawing a regular monthly salary. The salary should reflect in your bank account. To qualify for the loan, you must be employed with the current employer for at least 2 months.
  • 2. Is there a credit check done?

    Yes, we access your credit report/score when you apply for a small cash loan. Minimum CIBIL score needed is 600+. A lower score can be considered to provide loan in case your repayment capacity is strong.
  • 3. How do I send my bank statement and others documents?

    You can upload the soft copy of the documents when making the loan application. You do not have to send us a hard copy of any document. Alternatively, you can also email the documents at scl[at]loanbaba[dot]com
  • 4. How soon will the loan get approved?

    Lending decision is taken within minutes of receiving your small cash loan application. If you apply on Loanbaba on its non-working weekends, the application is then processed the next working day. Funds are typically disbursed within 2 hours to 24 hours of the loan approval.
  • 5. What are the late payment charges?

    You will be charged an addition of Rs. 100 per day, as late payment charges. Cheque bounce charges may apply. Try not to default at loan payments, since it will affect your credit score and weaken the chances of securing credit in the future from any financial institution and bank.
